Our Story

At our core, we are driven by a passion for purity and a commitment to creating organic sweets and snacks that are truly wholesome and completely free from chemicals, pesticides, and artificial additives. From the largest and most prominent sweets brands to small businesses use umpteen numbers of preservatives, chemicals, artificial colours, and additives to enhance the taste, shelf life, and aesthetics of their products. You may or may not be aware that several sweet makers use a chemical as a whitener in making Kaju Katli. It’s very common for the manufacturers to use red, green, and yellow colours in making Besan Laddoo, Bundi Laddoo, and Motichoor Laddoo. Several new researches in Europe and USA have demonstrated that numerous preservatives, colours, and oils that are used in the food preparation today have severe adverse effects on human health that cause serious ailments in the long term such as heart diseases and cancer. Some of them are already banned in Europe and some are being considered to be banned in the USA. Though some of the major brands publish the ingredients used in their products, which may not even publish the full list of the ingredients, it’s hard to find what many other small businesses are using the ingredients in their products. We had been on a hunt to find pure sweets and snacks as our grandmothers used to make at home. Tired of seeing the rampant use of chemicals, additives, preservatives, and fillers in the sweet industry, we set out on a mission to offer a better alternative. We believe that what you eat should be as pure and natural as possible, which is why we use only the finest organic ingredients in our products. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea – to provide consumers with organic sweets and snacks that are free from any harmful chemicals or fillers. We believe that everyone deserves to enjoy delicious treats without compromising their health, which is why we have dedicated ourselves to crafting products that are not only tasty but also truly wholesome. Join us on our mission to redefine the sweet industry and experience the pure joy of indulging in organic treats that are good for you and the planet. Together, we can create a sweeter, healthier future for all.”
